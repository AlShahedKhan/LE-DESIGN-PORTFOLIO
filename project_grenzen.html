/ 01 CONTEXT

8,920 km, 6 hours and continents apart

Basing off a theme in my personal life, the short-film “Grenzen” was created, portraying the borders between my parents, who live in Laos, and myself, in Switzerland. Through showing a typical day in the life of the three protagonists, a storyline is created in which the contrasts as well as the similarities in the separate lives are presented.
