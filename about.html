Leila Epprecht

MIXED-MEDIA DESIGNER

Leila Epprecht is a multidisciplinary designer specialising in 3D design and animation. Currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Visual Communication and Digital Environments in Basel, she is passionate about exploring the creative potentials within the digital realm. Having lived in a number of countries as well as having a diverse background, she has developed a strong ability to adapt to new environments, which informs her perspective and provides inspiration for her work. Leila aims to push the boundaries of digital design, integrating cultural influences to create work that is both unique and impactful.
